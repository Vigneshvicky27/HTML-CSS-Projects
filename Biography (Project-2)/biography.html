A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

1932-2015

Missile Man of Bharat

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, better known as A. P. J. Abdul Kalam who was the 11th president of India. Kalam was born and brought up in Rameswaram and became a scientist and worked for DRDO and ISRO. After his sudden demise at Shillong on 27th July 2015, he was laid to rest at Pei Karumbu, Rameswaram on 30th July 2015. A memorial has been built at the burial site. Inside, the memorial, one can see the selected photos, paintings and miniature models of misssiles etc.,. The memorial is less than 1 km from Rameswaram.

Biographies

  • Dr. A. P.J. Abdul Kalam was born in the Dhanushkodi village of Rameswaram which is now located in Tamil Nadu.
  • Dr. A. P.J. Abdul Kalam belonged to a poor family and used to sell newspapers to complete his studies.
  • Dr. A. P.J. Abdul Kalam completed his post-graduation in space science from the Madras Institute of Technology.
  • After completing his studies, Dr. A. P.J. Abdul Kalam started working as an aerospace engineer in the Defense Research and Development Organization (DRDO).
